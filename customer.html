Overview

The Device Compatibility Guide provides information about which electronic devices can be connected and information about how to connect them.

The Services Cube app is supported by a wide range of iOS (Apple) and Android devices. Most devices less than two years old should work well. However, it is not compatible with Windows devices at this time.

Services Cube is supported on the following operating systems:

Please note, you may not find a specific device on this page, as new devices update frequently. The Services Cube app can be downloaded from the corresponding app stores ( Google Play and Apple App Store ).

Creating Repeating Cases

Overview

Have you ever in the situation that you really need trust-worthy cleaner to clean up your house regularly but ended up hiring someone from the black-market and lasting less than a month? With the Services Cube APP, days like that are totally behind you.

Here's what you should do:

Basic Steps


  1. Choose the service you want in the home screen.
  2. Choose your Address and Phone Number.
  3. Select your Service Type.
  4. Choose "Daily / Weekly / Monthly" as you like in the Frequency area.
  5. Choose how many weeks you'd like to book.
  6. Choose how many hours you want for one time in the Duration area.
  7. Choose Time and Date for your service.
  8. Just click "Submit" and we'll be in touch with you real soon.

Note:

  • "First Start Date" means the start date of the first service. We'll automatically calculate the rest of the schedules based on that.
  • For weekly / daily services, Minimum time for booking is one week. As for monthly services, Minimum time for booking is one month.

Cases Calendar

Overview

Description

Services Cube is built for simplicity. We understand that complex scheduling can put customers in a pickle but it's no longer a problem anymore with the Services Cube APP.

The "cases" screen defaults to today's case schedules in a timeline. From this view, you can see all of your case cards with different case status in the calendar form.

Contents

Calendar view

The calendar area contains the following:

  • A left arrow button at the top-left corner.Tapping this button sets to the week / month before now.
  • A right arrow button at the top-right corner.Tapping this button sets to the week / month after now.
  • Pull down the screen reveals case calendar on a month-by-month basis.

Case cards

Case cards view contains the following types of information:

  • Case status (Case status changes through the whole process)
  • Case number (Each case has its own unique Sequence Number)
  • Case reporter (Customer who reported the case before)
  • Case address (The place where the case will be performed)
  • Case type (The specific type of service you required)
  • Case frequency (Once / Real-time / Daily / Weekly / Monthly)
  • Case start-time (Scheduled start time)
  • Case end-time (Scheduled end time)

Case details

Overview

Description

You can easily access case details by tapping the case card. The case details view shows the following information:

  • Map & location

    On our case map, you can view the detailed location information including the precise distance between you and your workers and the detailed routes after your workers arrived. Case map makes it easy to keep track of your workers and control the working progress.

  • Contact

    The contacting area is where you can contact your worker through the entire process. The left button is for sending messages and the right button is for making phone calls. No more worrying about unsufficient communication with your workers.

  • Case information

    Name, address, phone, service type, time, assignee. Basically, all the information you'll ever need.

  • Case progress

    Get notified of new case changes. The app will track all time periods as they progress through the job. As your assigned workers complete your work step by step, case status will also be updated.

  • Job notes

    Get detaild job notes about your case through the working process. You can also communicate with your worker about your detailed requests here.

  • Assets

    Get informed of all used assets.

  • Billings

    Keep you aware of all the expenditures. No hidden fees.
