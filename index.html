Mobile apps for admins

Our mobile applications for admins focus on scheduling and dispatching.

For each job, employees are automatically listed according to employee skills, scores, employee certifications,availability and travel time. Furthermore, admins are able to visually manage all jobs and workforce data-like locations, work history, and certifications on the real-time map.

There’s much more management features in our web app for companies - accounts management, services management, jobs management, invoices management,etc. You name it, we got it.
