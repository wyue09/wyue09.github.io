services cube

Built For Field-based Services

Powerful & Easy-to-use

Services cube is in the business of getting qualified field-based service providers for various services ranging from cleaning services, security needs, appliances repairs,gardening and so on.

Services cube makes it easy for scheduling,assigning jobs,tracking productivity.

Main Features

Mobile & Web apps

Services Cube is specially designed for three kinds of users, including customers, workers and company admins.

Yes, they all have their own access to Services Cube. Not only did this make it easier for your customers to reach you, but also greatly improving efficiency and saving labor and material costs.

And this is how it works. With simple clicks, customers can easily apply for services from their phone. Next, company admins assign skilled workers to jobs in the app. After that, workers are able to hit the road and complete the job using the app. Company admins can track, manage, and schedule their people from the mobile and web apps the whole time.

Main Features

Scheduling & Dispatching

With Services Cube, manual scheduling days are totally behind you. Seamless scheduling & powerful dispatching system makes it possible to manage your workforce intelligently.

You can select the right employee for each job based on job requirements, employee skills, employee certifications,availability and travel time. Saves your time and makes the control process much easier and much more efficient.

Main Features

Time tracking & Real-time map

Services Cube will track all time periods as they progress through the job.

Meanwhile,we offer real-time map and location tracking to help you better understand where your team is and when work is delivered.

No more manual delays and errors.

Main Features

Invoicing & Payments

With our centralized billing and online invoice system, invoices are created automatically after finishing the jobs and all payments are made via credit card.

You can also set discount level and tax rate for each different type of service.

Main Features

Job notes & Assets

Quickly and easily get detaild job notes including photo uploads and more through the working process . You can also communicate with your worker right in the app.

Assign assets to certain jobs and manage your warehouse inventory.

Main Features

Workforce management

Full-featured dashboard gives valuable insights and a more accurate picture about your mobile workforce.

Centrally and visually manage all your workers (full-time/part-time/contract) and their key data including certifications, availability, locations, skills, work history, etc.
