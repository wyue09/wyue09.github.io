Differentiator 1: Real Time Intelligence Augmentation

Intelligent Prediction differentiates our Architecture from the all others.

The system augments the human to determine which worker, customer, asset etc is best suited to execute for any given situation, based on the highest aggregate, cumulative rating for that specific type of situation, for a specific zip code, at a specific time, in combination with other worker(s), all in real time

Differentiator 2: Ai Functionality

Initial versions of your software will be based on rigid “If this then that” coding (see above), later versions will include deep machine learning, specifically Deep Convolutional Nets (DCNs) that will recognize previously unknown patterns; once your data training set grows rich enough the System will prioritize DCNs rather than the rigid coding; this is necessary as mountains of tagged and diverse data is needed for DCNs to function. Until then, the rigid codified business logic will apply so you are covered every step of the way.

In addition, the System will learn what action, or combinations, will produce optimal results for you based on the aggregate of all customers’ transactions, holistically, so the System as a whole will produce better results than any one customer’s system could in seclusion.

In addition,in the meantime, we will use the data generated from the “If this than that’ coding as a feeder for DCNs so the data and time will be optimized

The ultimate goal is for the System to proactively predict when an important event will occur (customer will need service, what type of service, the duration of the service, where, which combination of workers should be assigned, etc) and…. once approved by the human being... to execute. The human being will transition from initiating an action with support of the System to being a reviewer and approver of the System’s actions
